Sadullah Göncü was born in Istanbul and completed his high school education at Bakırköy Imam Hatip High School. In 2017, he graduated from Fatih Sultan Mehmet Vakıf University, Department of Civil Engineering. In 2019, he completed his Master's degree at Yıldız Technical University, Department of Civil Engineering, Transportation Program and was awarded the title of Master Engineer. Since 2020, he has been continuing his PhD education at Istanbul Technical University, Transportation Engineering Program.

Sadullah Göncü, who also worked in the private sector during his undergraduate education, has been working as a Research Assistant at Fatih Sultan Mehmet Foundation University, Civil Engineering Department since 2017.
