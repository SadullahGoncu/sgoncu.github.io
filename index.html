Sadullah Göncü was born in Istanbul, Turkey, in 1995. He received the M.Sc. degree in transportation engineering from Yıldız Technical University and the Ph.D. degree in transportation engineering from Istanbul Technical University in 2019 and 2025, respectively, and is a member of the ITU Intelligent Transportation Systems Research Laboratory.

Having worked in the private sector during his undergraduate education, Sadullah has served as a Research Assistant at Fatih Sultan Mehmet Vakıf University, Civil Engineering Department, since 2017.
